Controls
============================================
W,A,S,D to move character
Up,Down,Left,Right to change character view
When talking to NPC, press Y/N

E to interact with door
Z/X to turn off and on the lights and to change skybox 
(Lights used = point and spot lights)

============================================
Interactions available
============================================
Talking to santa
Talking to steve
Opening door
Take can of coke
Talking to vending machine
